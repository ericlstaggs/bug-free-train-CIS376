Our Mission

We are on a mission to make industrial environments safer and more efficient. By merging advanced thermodynamics with 3D printing technology, we are developing a new generation of deflagration arrestors that provide superior protection at a fraction of the traditional cost.

The Problem: Outdated Protection

Traditional deflagration arrestors—critical devices designed to stop subsonic flame fronts in piping systems—have remained largely unchanged for decades. They are often:

  • Cost-Prohibitive: High manufacturing and material costs for complex internal geometries.
  • Difficult to Maintain: Prone to clogging and require frequent, labor-intensive cleaning.
  • Inefficient: Bulky designs can cause significant pressure drops, reducing overall system performance.
